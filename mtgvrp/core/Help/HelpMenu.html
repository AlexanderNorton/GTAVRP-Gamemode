Command Syntax Information
/me /me [message]

message: Your roleplay message.

Used for desciribing actions of your character.
/give /give [id] [item] [amount]

id: Name or Id of target player.
item: Short name of the item, found in between [ ] in /inv.
amount: The amount of that item you'd like to give.

 Give an item to a nearby player from your inventory.
